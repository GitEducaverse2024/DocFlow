**Three resolution options for the planner/executor to choose:**
a. **Volume mount**: add `- ./.docflow-kb:/docflow-kb` and set `KB_ROOT=/docflow-kb` in env.

c. **Entrypoint copy**: `cp -r /app/.docflow-kb-seed /docflow-kb` in docker-entrypoint.sh, after COPY in Dockerfile. Similar to existing `data/knowledge` pattern.

**Recommended: Option (a) — volume mount + KB_ROOT env var.** Simplest, matches how `${HOME}/docflow-data` is done, updates to KB reflected without rebuild.
