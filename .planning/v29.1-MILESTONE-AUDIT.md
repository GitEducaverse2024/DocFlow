## Executive Summary

Phase 156 closed the 4 undeclared-scope gaps from the first audit (canvas sync, delete_catflow, link tools, orphans) by adding REQ-IDs KB-40..KB-43 and shipping the implementations. Structurally, v29.1 now delivers what its product claim promises: **KB is consumed by CatBot exclusively via `search_kb` + `get_kb_entry` + `list_*` tools, and every create/update/delete on the 6 canonical entities (catpaws, skills, connectors, catbrains, email-templates, canvases) goes through `syncResource` hooks.**

However, a post-Phase-156 integration check discovered a **REGRESSION in KB-43's invariant** caused by a sequencing bug in Phase 156 itself:

- Commit `c6e4ab6` (22:26) archived 15 orphans to `.docflow-legacy/orphans/`.
- Commit `06d69af7` (22:54) ran `kb-sync.cjs --full-rebuild --source db` to backfill `search_hints` for KB-42 — and in doing so, reintroduced 10 of the 15 archived orphans because `populateFromDb` does not treat `.docflow-legacy/orphans/` as an exclusion list.
- Phase 156 VERIFICATION.md (written after `06d69af7`) acknowledges the drift in a footnote but asserts "5/6 entities per-entity invariant == holds in runtime" — the current filesystem state shows only 1/6 holds, contradicting the VERIFICATION claim.

KB-42 is **PARTIAL** because the body-section renderer (`renderLinkedSection` + `replaceOrAppendSection` in `knowledge-sync.ts`) fires on every `syncResource('catpaw','update',...)` call for NEW operations but was never run over pre-existing linked CatPaws (~29 of them). Their frontmatter has `search_hints` but their Markdown bodies lack the "## Conectores vinculados" / "## Skills vinculadas" sections.

Closing v29.1 in current state would ship a milestone whose own KB-43 SUCCESS CRITERION ("active_kb_count == db_row_count for all 6 entities") is false in the working tree.
